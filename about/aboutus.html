The Journey of ShipDeckKK

Where Dreams Set Sail

Established in 2025, ShipDecKK was born from a passion for excellence in the maritime industry. From humble beginnings, we grew rapidly, building a reputation for delivering high-end yachts, cargo ships, and luxury liners to clients around the world.
Today, ShipDecKK stands as a trusted name, recognized for quality, craftsmanship, and outstanding customer service. Our team of shipbuilding experts and service specialists works closely with clients to create vessels that meet the highest standards of performance, design, and comfort.
